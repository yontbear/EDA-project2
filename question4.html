According to the literature (http://www.epa.gov/ttn/chief/net/2008neiv3/2008_neiv3_tsd_draft.pdf), there are four sectors (EI.Sector) dealing with Coal combustion: Fuel Comb - Comm/Institutional - Coal Fuel Comb - Electric Generation - Coal Fuel Comb - Industrial Boilers, ICEs - Coal Fuel Comb - Residential - Other In SCC$SCC.Level.Three we can also find coal.

#grepl key word "Coal" from SCC EI.Sector&SCC.Level.Two&SCC.Level.Three
coal1 <- SCC[grepl("Coal|Residential - Other",SCC$EI.Sector),]
nrow(coal1)#nrow of coal1
## [1] 103
grep("Coal",SCC$SCC.Level.Two)
## integer(0)
#no result of leveltwo
coal2 <- SCC[grepl("Coal",SCC$SCC.Level.Three),]
nrow(coal2) #nrow of coal2
## [1] 172
#collect all scc number
coal <- rbind(coal1,coal2)
scc <- coal$SCC
